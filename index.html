About Me

I am a life aficionado, intrigued by the fine details in the broad scheme of life. Through the years I have learned to quickly grasp complicated concepts and simplify them to communicate in a way that people can easily understand.

I believe in self development whether studying or reading and constantly investing time to understand where I can make the best contribution in the world. I am passionate about entrepreneurship and I always aspire to build businesses that solve problems and service relevant markets in an innovative way.

I believe in Africa it's potential and it's youth and how we can make a difference to fullfil our generational mandate.
